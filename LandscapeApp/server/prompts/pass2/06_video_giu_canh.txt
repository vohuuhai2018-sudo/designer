Use the uploaded image as the ONE AND ONLY visual reference.

PRIMARY TASK:
Generate a short, ultra-photorealistic video
by extending this real-life photograph in time,
as if it were recorded by a stationary or gently moving camera on site.

The scene already exists in real life.
This task is NOT to redesign, reinterpret, or enhance the scene,
but ONLY to introduce natural, physically plausible motion.

====================================================
ABSOLUTE CONSTRAINTS — NON-NEGOTIABLE
====================================================

- Do NOT add any new objects, elements, plants, stones, people, animals, light sources, or background details
- Do NOT remove or hide any existing elements
- Do NOT change geometry, layout, scale, proportions, distances, or spatial relationships
- Do NOT modify the design, composition, or functional intent of the scene
- Do NOT reconstruct or imagine parts that are not visible
- Do NOT extend, complete, or infer occluded or cropped elements
- Preserve the exact camera framing, perspective, and composition

UNKNOWN OR PARTIALLY VISIBLE AREAS:
- If any object is partially visible or occluded, do NOT guess or complete its shape
- Preserve ambiguity exactly as in the original image
- Unknown geometry must remain unknown

====================================================
ALLOWED MOTION (REAL-WORLD PHYSICS ONLY)
====================================================

Only the following natural motions are allowed:

WATER:
- Continuous, gravity-driven waterfall flow
- Subtle water surface ripples
- Natural reflection and refraction changes
- Soft foam movement where water contacts stone
- No exaggerated splashes or stylized motion

VEGETATION:
- Very subtle leaf and branch movement caused by light wind
- Natural, irregular motion (not synchronized)
- No bending, stretching, or reshaping of plants

LIGHT & SHADOW:
- Gentle, realistic changes in light reflection
- Soft shadow movement consistent with outdoor daylight
- No dramatic lighting shifts

ATMOSPHERE:
- Calm outdoor environment
- No fog, mist, smoke, rain, or additional weather effects

====================================================
STRICTLY FORBIDDEN MOTION
====================================================

- No camera shake
- No cinematic camera moves
- No dolly, crane, orbit, or zoom unless extremely subtle and realistic
- No artificial slow motion
- No dramatic parallax
- No depth hallucination
- No object deformation

====================================================
CAMERA BEHAVIOR
====================================================

- Camera must feel like a real tripod or handheld camera at rest
- If any movement exists:
  - extremely slow
  - physically plausible
  - minimal micro motion only
- No wide-angle distortion
- No cinematic framing
- No stylized motion blur

====================================================
MATERIAL & SURFACE BEHAVIOR
====================================================

- Stone surfaces must remain rigid and unchanged
- Wood must remain static with only light interaction
- Glass (if present) must show subtle reflection changes only
- No material morphing or enhancement

====================================================
STYLE & QUALITY
====================================================

- Ultra-photorealistic real-life video
- Natural color, neutral tone
- No HDR exaggeration
- No cinematic grading
- No artificial enhancement

====================================================
FINAL OUTPUT REQUIREMENT
====================================================

The resulting video must look like
a real video recording taken at the actual location,
with the camera capturing natural environmental motion only.

If any element appears added, altered, guessed, or creatively interpreted,
the result is incorrect.
